About

I'm Roemie Osias, a design student at San Francisco State University. My work lives at the intersection of culture, community, and communication — whether that's building a brand identity for a century-old cultural hall, or documenting my family's migration story as a filterable archive.

I'm drawn to projects that carry meaning beyond aesthetics: design that preserves something, connects someone, or changes how a story is told.
